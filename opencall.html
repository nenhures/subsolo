subsolo

open call

subsolo is calling all visual artists to submit their work for the upcoming web-based group show CUTOUT.

Selected artists will have their work displayed on subsolo's website and social media and have the opportunity to participate in future interviews, art talks, collaborations and other web and social media actions.

cutout

Composing and rearranging things are crucial means of constructing images and ideas. For subsolo's first edition, a group of artists will be selected to participate in CUTOUT, a digital show and experience in which fragments of the artists' works shall be displayed in an interactive web platform that will allow viewers to build new and unique arrangements, and thus transform the artist's work in unpredictable ways.

submission

To enter the open call, you must submit the following to subsolo.net@gmail.com:

    - Portfolio, online or PDF (maximum 15MB);
    - C.V.;
    - Short bio and artist's statement (maximum 300 words).

You are welcome to make your submission either in english or in portuguese.

purpose

- Show art digitally in unconventional ways;

- Increase the artists' visibility with a non-physical approach to exhibiting art;

- Create connections between artists of different places and backgrounds, as well as with a global audience;

- Establish the viewer as part of the creation and transformation of artwork through an interactive approach;

- Encourage art practice and discourse in a time of social distancing.

deadline

Submission deadline: February 15, 23:59 GMT.

Selected artists will be contacted until February 21.

Exhibition predicted launch: March 6.

terms

Any artist at least 18 years old from any country or background is welcome to participate. Accepted media for submission includes: painting, drawing, photography, sculpture, installation, and other visual media. Due to the online nature of the show, no physical works will be required, so the submited work must be adequately photographed.

The artist agrees to have their work's images digitally transformed and used in subsolo.net and subsolo's social media.

Selected artists will be contacted through e-mail.

Selected artists will be asked for high-resolution images of their work.

There are no submission fees.

about

subsolo is a web-based project that aims to show various artists' works in new interactive ways.

As we are faced with a new paradigm that demands different non-physical modes of exhibiting art, the digital space is now more than ever a tool to connect artists to their audiences. subsolo is a platform for creating and showing art in unpredictable ways through the use of digital means, connecting artists and their work with a global audience.

Each edition will include different artists that will be challenged to transform or create work that interlaces their practice with the web. The audience is able to view, interact and transform the art remotely.

subsolo is an artist-run project. The founder and curator is the artist Rafaela Nunes, also known as Nenhures.
